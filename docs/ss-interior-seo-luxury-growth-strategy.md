## 3. Complete Content Strategy

SS Interior should build topical authority around restoration, materials, and use cases. Every page should explain the decision-making behind preservation: condition assessment, material selection, upholstery craft, comfort rebuilding, leather and textile behavior, commercial durability, and room context.

Priority content clusters:

- Sofa restoration.
- Leather restoration.
- Bespoke upholstery.
- Furniture reupholstery.
- Commercial and hospitality restoration.
- Material care and maintenance.
- Restore vs replace decisions.
- Before/after transformation storytelling.

The tone should read like a luxury interiors publication. Avoid local-service vocabulary. Use phrases such as atelier review, material direction, hand-finished seams, heritage preservation, renewed comfort, and transformation study.
