# Premium Pricing Architecture

**ORYN** operates on the principle of transformation-based pricing. We do not sell hours. We do not sell websites. We sell the global perception of multi-million dollar architectural assets.

## 1. Transformation-Based Pricing
- **The Paradigm Shift:** We charge based on the value of the asset we are elevating. A digital ecosystem that sells out a $500M luxury development is worth significantly more than standard market rates.
- **The Framing:** We frame our fee as an investment in the property's aura, directly impacting its perceived market value and sales velocity.

## 2. Exclusivity Pricing Systems
- **The Premium:** Our prices naturally filter out clients who do not understand luxury. High prices create an immediate psychological barrier that establishes authority and commands respect.
- **No Line Items:** We do not provide itemized lists of "1x Homepage, 3x Revisions." We provide a single, monumental figure for "The Ecosystem."

## 3. Premium Retainer Structures
- **Digital Estate Management:** Following launch, we offer a high-ticket retainer. This is not "maintenance." This is "Atmospheric Governance." We ensure the site remains perfectly optimized, update case studies cinematically, and manage the digital aura over time.

## 4. Luxury Consultation Pricing
- **Paid Diagnostics:** We do not perform free audits. Initial deep-dive architectural diagnostics are paid engagements. If they choose to proceed with the ecosystem, the fee is credited. This immediately establishes authority.

## 5. Premium Negotiation Frameworks
- **The Walkaway Power:** We never discount. If a client challenges the fee, we do not negotiate the price; we negotiate the scope, or we gracefully decline the project. Luxury does not haggle.
